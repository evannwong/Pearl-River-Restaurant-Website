About Us

We are a fully licensed Cantonese and Szechuan restaurant founded in 19xx, and currently owned by four partners. We are a local business and have been serving Chinese food to Edmonton for many years, located in South Edmonton at 4728 - 99 Street NW.

You can find many traditional dishes such as our Cantonese Chow Mein, as well as newer takes on Chinese food such as our Sweet and Sour Pork Spareribs.
